Quote of the Day

“An army of sheep lead by a lion can defeat an army of lions lead by a sheep”- African Proverb This quote means the team matters but the leader matters mostly, Strong leader means strong team weak leader means weak team. “He who thinks he is leading and has no one following him is only taking a walk”- Malawian Proverb Because you can be a good leader but without any followers you aren’t leading anything.
